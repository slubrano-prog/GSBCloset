Common questions
Who can join?

Right now, anyone with a Stanford email (@stanford.edu or @gsb.stanford.edu), plus their direct invitees. We're starting with GSB '26 and '27 and expanding from there.

Do I have to upload to borrow?

For the beta, yes — at least one piece. The whole thing only works if everyone contributes. After your first borrow, you can decide if you want to keep your closet open.

What if a dress comes back damaged?

You agree on terms directly with the lender. We recommend a $50–100 damage deposit for high-value pieces. Long-term we're exploring a lender-protection fund.

How is this different from Pickle or Rent the Runway?

Pickle is open to strangers and hard to filter. Rent the Runway carries inventory and charges retail-adjacent prices. The Inner Closet is your friends, your network, and the cost of dry cleaning.

How do payments work?

Through Venmo, directly between you and the lender. We don't process payments and we don't take a cut.

Is my closet visible to everyone?

No. Only your direct friends and friends-of-friends in the network can see it. Strangers — even other GSB students you don't know — can't.
